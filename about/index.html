About

Abdul
Rehman

Based in Pakistan. Joined Web3 when I was 14 driven by curiosity and a desire to understand how this technology could reshape the future. Aptos was one of the first ecosystems I explored, which later led me into the Sui ecosystem.

Over the years I worked as a CM across 50+ projects including Suivivors, GalacticApes, Sui Bears, and many others. Currently pursuing a Bachelor's in Computer Science.

I spend most of my time learning, reading, writing, researching, and connecting with people who share the same passion. Alongside that, I'm also a Web3 artist who enjoys turning ideas into creative work.

Currently community lead for Pakistan at Monad, MegaETH, and Morph, helping grow local communities, support builders, and contribute to the infrastructure that truly matters. Was a pleasure to host the Monad IRL event for the Pakistan community.

I believe great ecosystems are built by the people behind them, and I'm grateful to be part of that journey.
